Frequently Asked Questions

How do I link an elder and caregiver account?

Login as Elder → Home tab → "Link Caregiver" button → Copy the 6-digit code. Then login as Caregiver → Enter the code to connect.

Do I need a wearable device to use ElderCare?

No, wearables are optional. You can manually log health vitals or use the built-in mock data generator for testing.

How do automated alerts work?

The app monitors vitals and sends RED alerts for heart rate <60 or >100 bpm, and AMBER alerts for sleep <5 hours. Caregivers receive push notifications instantly.

Is my health data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and stored securely in Firebase. Only linked caregivers can access your health information.

What happens when I press the SOS button?

A 3-second countdown starts, then an emergency alert with your GPS location is sent to all linked caregivers via SMS and push notification.

Can I use ElderCare offline?

Limited functionality is available offline (viewing cached data), but real-time sync and alerts require an internet connection.

How do medication reminders work?

Set reminder times for each medication. The app will show a full-screen alarm notification at the scheduled time.

Can one caregiver monitor multiple elders?

Yes, caregivers can link to multiple elder accounts and monitor all of them from a single dashboard.

Is ElderCare really free?

Yes, completely free with no subscriptions or hidden fees. It's open source under the MIT License.

What Android version do I need?

Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher is required.

Common Issues

App won't install

Enable "Install from unknown sources" in Android Settings → Security. Make sure you have Android 7.0 or higher.

Notifications not working

Check Android Settings → Apps → ElderCare → Notifications. Ensure all notification channels are enabled.

Can't link accounts

Ensure both devices have internet connection. The 6-digit code expires after 10 minutes - generate a new one if needed.

Vitals not syncing

Check Firebase configuration (google-services.json). Verify internet connection and that accounts are properly linked.
